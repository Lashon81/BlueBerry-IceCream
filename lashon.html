Oatmeal Raisin Cookies

Oatmeal raisin cookies typically contain butter, flour, sugar (both granulated and brown), eggs, oats, raisins, and spices like cinnamon. Some recipes may also include salt, baking soda, vanilla extract, and other flavorings

How to make Oatmeal Raisins Cookies?

  1. 1/2 Cup(s) (1 stick) plus 6 tablespoons butter, softened
  2. 3/4 Cup(s) firmly packed brown sugar
  3. 1/2 Cup(s) granulated sugar
  4. 2 Eggs
  5. 1 Teaspoon(s) vanilla
  6. 1-1/2 Cup(s) all-purpose flour
  7. 1 Teaspoon(s) Baking Soda
  8. 1 Teaspoon(s) ground cinnamon
