My Favorite Oatmeal Raisin Cookies

These oatmeal raisins cookies are chewy, warm, and full of flavor. I love oatmeal raisin cookies with just the right amount of cinnamon and a handful of raisins. Oatmeal raisin cookies are a classic cookie known for their chewy texture, sweet and sometimes slightly tangy flavor from the raisins, and warm, comforting taste. They are often seen as a slightly healthier alternative to chocolate chip cookies due to the oats and raisins providing fiber.

How to make Oatmeal Raisin Cookies?

  • 1/2 Cup(s) (1 stick) plus 6 tablespoons butter, softened
  • 3/4 Cup(s) firmly packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 Cup(s) granulated sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Teaspoon(s) vanilla
  • 1-1/2 Cup(s) all-purpose flour
  • 1 Teaspoon(s) Baking Soda
  • 1 Teaspoon(s) ground cinnamon
